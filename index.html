About Me

I'm Owain West, a current Sophomore at the University of Pennsylvania studying Logic, Information, and Computation.

I'm tremendously interested in the possibility of true Artificial Intelligence, love the complete internal consistency of well designed languages (like Python - and OCaml!), and am nowhere more at home than a hackathon.

Beyond that, I am a serial photographer, a budding audiophile, and a perpetual bathrobe-wearer-extraordinaire. I write for a newspaper, work for a bank, audit classes in my free time, and play on the Penn Table Tennis Team.
